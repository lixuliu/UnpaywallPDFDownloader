UnpaywallPDFDownloader: Automated Open Access PDF Fetcher

UnpaywallPDFDownloader is a Python-based research tool for automating the download of open-access research articles in PDF format using DOIs from a CSV file. It leverages the Unpaywall API to streamline scholarly communication and open access workflows, supporting researchers and librarians in managing academic publications efficiently.

  • Fetches open-access PDFs by DOI from a CSV file
  • Tracks progress and logs errors
  • Handles missing DOIs, failed downloads, and unavailable open-access versions
  • Plays a notification sound upon completion
  • Openly licensed (CC BY 4.0) for academic and commercial use with attribution
  • Developed and published by the University of Birmingham, School of Engineering
